## About Me

I build tools for the retro computing community and love working close to the hardware. My main focus is **Amiga emulation**, where I maintain one of the most popular open-source emulators. I also enjoy working with APIs, automation, and anything that bridges old and new tech.

## Amiberry — My Flagship Project

<div align="center">

<a href="https://github.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry">
<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry/master/data/amiberry-logo.png" alt="Amiberry Logo" width="200">
</a>

**An optimized, multi-platform Amiga emulator**

[![Stars](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/BlitterStudio/amiberry?style=for-the-badge&color=f5c542)](https://github.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry/stargazers)
[![Forks](https://img.shields.io/github/forks/BlitterStudio/amiberry?style=for-the-badge&color=4287f5)](https://github.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry/network/members)
[![Release](https://img.shields.io/github/v/release/BlitterStudio/amiberry?style=for-the-badge&color=42f587)](https://github.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry/releases)
[![License](https://img.shields.io/github/license/BlitterStudio/amiberry?style=for-the-badge&color=999)](https://github.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry/blob/master/LICENSE)

</div>

Amiberry runs on **Linux** (x86_64, ARM, AArch64), **macOS** (Intel & Apple Silicon), **Windows**, and **Android**. It features JIT compilation, CRT shaders, WHDLoad integration, and is the go-to emulator for Raspberry Pi and other SBCs.

> [**amiberry.com**](https://amiberry.com) | [GitHub](https://github.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry) | [Wiki](https://github.com/BlitterStudio/amiberry/wiki)
